Shirts

Each shirt is individually selected and from there the shirt itself decides the feel and form of its pattern and covering.

As I draw directly onto the shirt using my fabric pen, the shirt gets to retain its natural softness, ready for everyday and every moment use.

The designs are all hand drawn and then heat set so that you can wear the shirt as you would any other
