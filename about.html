-My Name is Noi-

For the past few years, I have been drawing, playing and creating shapes on paper with my pen. After a decade of this I have decided to put my art to good use, creating a book with something for everyone.

I have heard about skilled potters, who just before finishing their piece, they would slide their thumb to add a kind of blemish, an imperfection, on the side as a method of making every piece unique. This story stuck with me and I always, intentionally or not, add unique and asymmetrical forms into my designs.

Drawing with only my pen directly onto the paper made my life a bit harder, but it gave me the opportunity to learn the art and philosophy of mistakes. Mistakes are part of life.

Fast fashion and art these days creates perfect pieces, but with no soul or movement. My hand drawn designs aim to do this, making each creation unique, and hopefully, a bright and helpful addition to your life.
